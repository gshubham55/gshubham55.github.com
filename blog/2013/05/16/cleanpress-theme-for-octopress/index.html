Cleanpress Theme for Octopress

After going through a heckload of themes, I short-listed a few of them, only to realise that my friends already had dibs on most of them.

Abhishek Kandoi used Fabric for his blog. Shashank Mehta used greyshade which he designed himself (impressive!).

Later I came across cleanpress which is an “elegant, clean & white minimal and responsive theme for Octopress.”

It was clean as promised and a candy for the eye (girly, is it?). It became an instant choice.
